How to destress.

Stressed out? Use these simple remidies at home or on the go.

Although we try very hard not to get stressed out at all, we all get stressed out sometime. Try these remedies that will calm you down immediately.

How to destress on the go.

1.) Listen to something calming. A favorite is the sound of water.

2.) Get your favorite drink. It might sound like something that you do all the time, but treating yourself to something could help you feel a lot better.

3.) This website works wonders. 'Stress Relief Pig' supplies many helpful tips and resources that you can use to calm yourself. It can be used at home or on the go. My personal favorite is the 'Games' page, because there are so many different games for many different emotions. My favorites are 'Home Sheep Home', 'Fishing Girl', 'Flower Reaction', 'Music Catch 2', and 'Destroy the Computer'.

How to destress at home.

1.) Make some herbal tea. You can use any recipe, but one that helps with sleep and reduces stress levels is chamomile tea with lavender and honey. For one pot, put 2 spoons of chamomile buds and 1 spoon of lavender buds in a tea strainer. Add hot water, and let all the flavor seep out of the buds before removing the strainer. Add a half spoon of honey (or less, depending on your preference). The honey is optional. This is a soothing drink that will make you feel calmer in no time.

2.) Stretch. Do some yoga.

3.) Excercise. It's healthy, and a good way to let your emotions out.

4.) Play some motivational music. Blast it loud and dance it out!

5.) Sleep it away. For some people, they have their a-ha moments when they wake up from a good sleep, and their heads are feeling much clearer.

6.) Play with your pet. If you have a pet, play with it! If you don't, don't feel left out, go to the dog park and meet some furry friends! Unless you have a cat that scratches, it's hard to be mad at a playful slobbering fluffball.

7.) Take a bath. A hot bath can be highly relaxing, especially if you add essential oils like lavender. Another great bath product id bath oils and bombs. LUSH is a great place that has all types of scented and colorful bath and shower products that are all handmade.

8.) Get a massage. This may require going out, and wil require some time. Slather on some essential oils, and let your muscles relax.

9.) If there's something you really need to do, face it! No better way to toss that stress away than to get it over with.
